Welcome!

We're so glad you joined us at the Museo di Maranello - a website for Ferrari enthusiasts, or just about anyone, to learn more about the history and legacy of the iconic prancing horse. This website is organised by time periods and you will find the links to each page by clciking in the top-right corner navigation menu. If you'd like to reach out to us with any ideas, comments, or suggestions, feel free to use our contact form found in the navigation menu.

Quick Fire Facts

  • Ferrari goes by the nickname "prancing horse".
  • Ferrari is a publically traded company under the ticker RACE.
  • Michael Schumacher a seven time F1 World Champion who raced with Ferrari was once the highest paid sportsman of all time.
