A Golden Era: 1970-1999

Some of Ferrari's most iconic cars come from this period of time. Cars like the F40, the 512, and the Testarossa may have been plastered upon your childhood bedrooms.

The F40: Enzo's Last Ferrari

The iconic and much-admired Ferrari F40 was the last Ferrari to receive Enzo Ferrari's personal approval. This mid-engine, rear-wheel-drive sports car was produced between the late 1980s to early 1990s. The F40 was the successor to the 288 GTO and was designed and built to celebrate Ferrari's 40th Anniversary. Only 400 were ever produced and each unit sold for around $400,000.
