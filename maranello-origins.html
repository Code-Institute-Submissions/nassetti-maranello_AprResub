Museo di Maranello

Humble Beginnings

Every story has to start somewhere and this one starts in 1929 with Scuderia Ferrari, or in English: The Ferrari Stable. Headquarted in Modena, Italy, Enzo Ferrari started out preparing Alfa Romeos for what they called "gentleman racers" until eventually he wanted to do his own thing. So in 1939 he did! As you can see below, that's when Maranello truly got on its feet and people started to see what Enzo could do.
